Students & Management Software

Student Management Software (SMS) is a digital solution designed to manage and track all aspects of student life, from enrollment to graduation. It helps educational institutions streamline student-related processes, track academic performance, manage communication, and ensure compliance with institutional regulations. The software typically includes features related to enrollment, attendance, grades, behavior management, and communication with parents and teachers.

School Management System Features

The School Management System streamlines academic and administrative tasks with features like online admission, student data management, attendance tracking, timetable scheduling, fee management, achievements tracking, and event planning. It ensures secure transactions, automated reports, and easy accessibility for students, teachers, and administrators.

  • Admission Management: Online registration, fee payment, merit list, and tracking.
  • Student Management: Centralized records, attendance, and performance tracking.
  • Time Table: Timetables for teachers & students, including exams & meals.
  • Attendance Management: Automated attendance, reports, and daily/yearly tracking.
  • Fees Management: Secure transactions, UPI/card payments, and fee alerts.
  • Achievements: Performance tracking and secure transaction management.
  • Event Management: School calendar, event tracking, and payment options.

Features

Student and Parent Portals

Secure portals where students can access assignments, grades, attendance records, and important announcements. Parents can view their child’s progress, attendance, and communicate with teachers.

Transport Tracking System

Integration of GPS-based tracking for school buses, allowing parents to monitor their child's journey. Notifications for pick-up and drop-off times, and route details for added convenience and safety.

Online Fee Payment and Billing

Integrate an online payment system for easy fee payment, receipt tracking, and automated billing reminders. Payment history and invoices can also be available for parents to track expenses.

Attendance Tracking and Reporting

A real-time attendance tracking system with daily or period-based attendance updates. Automated notifications for parents if a student is absent, and attendance reports for teachers and administrators.

Homework and Assignment Management

Teachers can post assignments, with due dates, resources, and instructions, directly on the portal. Students can submit assignments online, and teachers can provide feedback and grades.

Event Calendar and Announcements

A shared calendar that lists important dates for exams, school events, and holidays. Notifications for upcoming events or school-wide announcements to keep parents and students updated.
