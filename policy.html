EdShip Technologies Privacy Policy

At EdShip Technologies, protecting customer privacy is our top priority. We are committed to securing user information and complying with legal standards to safeguard your data. This policy explains the information we collect, how we use and share it, and how we protect your content. By using our services, you agree to this policy. For inquiries, contact us at info@edship.in or +91-6238506588.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information:

    This includes your name, email, contact details, job title, school affiliation (if applicable), and any voluntarily provided information.

  • Student Data (if applicable):

    We collect student data (names, attendance, grades, etc.) under strict confidentiality, with schools as data controllers and EdShip Technologies as the data processor.

  • Usage Data:

    We collect information about how you use the Website and Services, such as pages visited, features used, and time spent on the Website.

  • Location Data (if applicable):

    We collect location data for GPS tracking only with consent and solely for providing tracking services.

  • RFID Data (LF Proximity Cards):

    We collect RFID data with consent solely for tracking student and staff attendance.

How we use Information

We use your personal information to inform you about updates, events, and policy changes. Security details are used only for password recovery. Log data helps analyze trends, improve website navigation, and enhance our services. We also ensure secure access to our services via web, mobile, or API.

With whom we share Information

We may share aggregated, non-personal data with partners, affiliates, and advertisers. Personal information may be shared with business partners under this privacy policy. We also share data with service providers, legal authorities, and in business transfers.

Cookie Policy

We use temporary and permanent cookies to enhance website experience and enable easier access. Cookies help recognize your device, personalize features, simplify navigation, and analyze website usage. EdShip Technologies also uses Google Analytics to gather insights through cookies,

Links from our websites

Some pages of our websites contain external links. You are advised to verify the privacy practices of such other websites. We are not responsible for the manner of use or misuse of information made available by you at such other websites. We encourage you not to provide personal information, without assuring yourselves of the privacy policy.

How secure is your Information

While internet transmissions are not 100% secure, we take strong measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. Users must safeguard their passwords and report any security concerns. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel under confidentiality agreements.

Where We Store and Process Data

We store and process personal data on cloud platforms provided by U.S. companies such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Microsoft Azure. These platforms offer secure, scalable, and reliable infrastructure to ensure data protection.

Your Choice in Information Use

Registration requires personal information, and declining may limit service access. You cannot opt out of essential updates, but we seek consent for any different use of your data and provide opt-out options when applicable.

Accessing, Updating and Removing Personal Information

Upon request we provide users with access to their personally identifiable information free of charge. Users may correct, update or remove any such personally identifiable information either through the users’ profile or by contacting EdShip Technologies Customer Support Services. Such changes may take up to 48 hours to take effect.

Notification of Changes

We may modify this policy at any time and will indicate updates by displaying the last revised date. Changes will be posted on our website, and if we use personal data differently than originally stated, we will notify you via email with an opt-out option. Check EdShip Technologies Privacy Policy periodically for updates.

Customer rights

If you are accessing our Service from outside of the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. Your use of the Websites indicates your consent to the transfer of your information to our facilities anywhere across the world and as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

Global Data Processing: Your data may be processed in countries with different data protection laws than your own.
Security Measures: We implement strict security protocols to protect your data during international transfers.
Compliance with Regulations: We adhere to applicable data protection laws and standards, ensuring lawful data handling across borders.

Right of access to your personal information:

You have the right to ask us for confirmation on whether we are processing your personal information, and access to the personal information and related information on that processing (e.g., the purposes of the processing, or the categories of personal information involved).

Right to correction:

You have the right to have your personal information corrected, as permitted by law.

Right to erasure:

You have the right to ask us to delete your personal information, as permitted by law.

Right to restriction of processing:

You have the right to request the limiting of our processing under limited circumstances, including: when the accuracy of your personal information is contested; when the processing is unlawful and you oppose the erasure of your personal information and request the restriction of the use of your personal information instead; or when you have objected to processing pending the verification whether the legitimate grounds of EdShip Technologies override your grounds.

Right to data portability:

You have the right to receive the personal information that you have provided to us, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format, and you have the right to transmit that information to another controller, including to have it transmitted directly, where technically feasible.

Right to object:

You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information, as permitted by law.
