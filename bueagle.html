Bueagle

Student bus tracking software is a real-time tracking solution that helps parents, schools, and administrators monitor the location and status of school buses, ensuring timely pickups, enhanced safety, and improved communication.

Real Time GPS Tracking

• Live location tracking of school buses.
• Displays the bus's current location on a map interface for parents and school authorities.
• Provides accurate Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) for each stop.

Route Management

• Predefined routes and stop locations uploaded to the system.
• Alerts for deviations from the planned route.
• Optimization suggestions to improve fuel efficiency and reduce travel time.

Student Boarding Tracking

• Uses RFID cards, biometrics, or QR codes to log when students board or leave the bus.
• Sends instant notifications to parents about their child's status.

Two-Way Communication

• Enables direct communication between the driver, school administrators, and parents.
• Reduces delays and confusion during emergencies or schedule changes.

Attendance and Reporting

• Automatically logs student attendance during the bus ride.
• Generates daily, weekly, or monthly reports for schools and parents.
